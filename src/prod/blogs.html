I had hallucinations in the past
1/23/2025
Okay so this is kind of different story here, kind of late for the halloween mood but what ever.
Recently i came to a realization that what i thought was nothing, was actually something pretty big, i've always dissmised these events that i'm about to share, as a childish things, didn't gave much of a thought.
But then i connected some stuff, maybe what i was witnessing as a chlid, and the sigh im hearing, was all in my head.
Okay, starting from the earliest thing i remember, was a demonic voice calling my name and then laughs 4 times. I dont know why but I perfectly clearly remember the laugh. I can still hear it in my head if I try to remember it. The voice sounded like it was in my room. I was about 6-8 years old then.
Then, several years later, when i was about 12-14, i had what i thought poletergeist or some sort of ghosts activity. I heared literal running in the house, when i was home alome, not only that, but I heared as the ball dropped and it bounce several times. We had no ball whatsoever, and definetly not basketball or something that heavy.
Somewhere in between those years, I visited my cousin for summer vacation. He lived in the UK with his mother. He was a year older than me. I remember the first night, I was awoken, but i dont know why, and I see the curtains literally jumping up and down, left and right. I rubbed my eyes and checking if it was real (literally rubbed my eyes from disbelief). I then try to wake my cousin up, but when he awakes, the curtains stops moving. My eyes are glued to the curtains, I cant stop looking at them, but as soon as he tells me theres nothing there and goes to sleep, the curtains moves again. It happened for several times before i went to sleep (I cant remember past this point).
I remember then, some nights later, I had something simmilar happened, i dont know how i was awoken at night, i cant remember that, but I was seeing something in the window, that was a full view, and heared a witch-like laugh, then the shadow flew away. (Im not even believing myself as I'm writing this, thats how crazy it sounds).
From this point onwards im not aware that I hallucinated visually anymore, however I had some pretty noticeable audio hallucinations. I pretty often used to hear an audible sigh, coming from somewher in the house of. Of course i dismissed it as its definetly has to do something with the heating or the pipes. Didnt gave much thought, just annoyed me thats all. But recently, last year as of this writing, I heared THE SAME EXACT sigh in the office I was working from. At first I thought that was weird, but didnt gave much thought about it, until I made realization that its the same sound that annoyed me in the house. The pitch is the same... I dont know how to explain it, it was very distinct sigh...
I don't hear the sigh anymore, the office was the last place I heared it. Maybe it's nothing, I hope it's nothing and I'm overreacting.
Maybe I'm wrong, maybe it is just childish dreams and nothing more. I just recently made the posible connection myself, that the sigh might has something to do with my previous unexplained things like those 'ghost' stuff.
P.S these 'ghost' stuff happened 3 times in total, I don't wanna get into details very much, as it would reveal my fucked up childhood, where I lived in the foster home, had/have abusive parents, and other stuff that im not ready to talk about.
Im working on a multiplayer game
1/20/2025
I started to work on a multiplayer game. The game itself won't be for a full release, it's more for a learning purpose than actually releasing a full developed game. Of course, i want to make a functional game, but that all the game will be, just functional, for now.

The development of the game will serve the learning purpose. I'm looking to learn about game servers, how servers manage players input, and sending the response back to client. Basically server-client proccess of multiplayer game.
For server side development, I use C language, and let me tell you, developing server, or anything basically in pure C, is one hell of a thing to do. The amount stuff one have to do manually, is concerning. Now i will definetly appriaciate not having to deal with strings manually when i use anything else than C XD.

What im planning to achive:
  • Have the player enter its IGN when first entering the game.
  • Connect the player to the available session.
  • Have multiple players on the same game session.
  • Have players moving around and have the player movements showed to other players.

For a reference, im using maple story, an 2000s 2D multiplayer game. I like the simplistic style of it, and the fact that is 2D
